Stickman games encompass a diverse range of titles that feature simplistic stick-figure characters in various gameplay scenarios, including action, adventure, sports, and puzzle genres. Despite their simple appearance, stickman games can provide engaging and addictive gameplay experiences across a wide array of genres and platforms.
